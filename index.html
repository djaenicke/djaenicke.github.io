Education

I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering Technology from Purdue University in 2015. Before I was even finished with undergrad, I knew that I was interested in continuing my education. Due to a lack of graduate degree options in technology, I decided to pursue an engineering degree. This pursuit began in Fall of 2015 with taking Mathematics courses at Indiana University Kokomo. During this time, I applied for the Master of Science in in Mechanical Engineering program at Indiana University Purdue University Indianapolis. I was interested in studying control theory and had realized that most systems that are controlled require knowledge in basic mechanics, fluid dynamics, and heat transfer. For this reason, I decided to pursue Mechanical Engineering with a primary focus in mechantronics and controls and a secondary focus in systems engineering.
